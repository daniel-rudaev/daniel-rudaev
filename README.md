# Hi, I'm Daniel 👋

I help founders and operators move from operational chaos to clarity in 90 days — designing governance-first automation, AI systems, and operations stacks for cross-border teams.

Originally from Israel — working across Europe, the Gulf, and Southeast Asia. Running [D1DX](https://d1dx.com), an automations and AI studio. I got into this after running a real business — a pub — and experiencing the chaos firsthand.

---

### What I Do

- **Digital transformation** — governance-first ops overhauls with fixed 30–90 day delivery windows, KPI-linked Playbooks, and live dashboards. Built for 5–20 person businesses in Israel, the Gulf, and Southeast Asia.
- **AI agents & intelligent automation** — autonomous workflows, multi-agent pipelines, and human-in-the-loop systems using Make.com, n8n, and Claude. Built for production, not demos.
- **Compliance & governance** — privacy-by-design architecture for businesses navigating Israel Amendment 13, UAE PDPL, and GDPR. Lock-in-free, audit-ready, from day one.
- **Leadership & mentorship** — 1:1 and group coaching for non-technical founders and operators becoming data-literate leaders of cross-cultural teams
- **Custom systems** — Bubble apps · WhatsApp · Telegram · Chatwoot · Coolify self-hosted infrastructure · AWS & GCP Serverless · custom code when no-code isn't enough
