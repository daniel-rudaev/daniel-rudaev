# Daniel Rudaev

**From Chaos to Clarity in 90 Days.**

I design and lead governance-first digital transformations for small, cross-border businesses. Through my operations studio [D1DX](https://d1dx.com), I replace manual work and fragmented systems with low-code automation, integrated dashboards, and vetted human-in-the-loop support — delivering measurable ROI within 30-90 days.

---

### What I Do

**Digital Transformation Architecture**
I map real workflows, eliminate inefficiencies, and build compliance-ready operations stacks for 5-20 person businesses across Israeli, Gulf, and international markets. Every engagement follows a KPI-linked Playbook with fixed timelines and visible outcomes.

**Automation & Systems Design**
I integrate CRMs, analytics, marketing, support, and project management into a single, governed ecosystem. My approach blends low-code platforms with AI and a vetted Manual Automation Crew for work that is too messy or expensive to automate fully.

**Mentorship & Leadership Programs**
I run 1:1 mentorship focused on building digital leaders — helping non-technical founders and operators become confident, data-literate decision-makers who lead cross-cultural teams with clarity.

---

### How I Work

- **Compliance-first.** Privacy-by-design, audit-ready systems, and governance baked into every workflow — not bolted on after.
- **Proof, not theater.** 30-90 day Playbooks with clear KPIs, live dashboards, and quantified ROI. No slideware.
- **Lock-in-free.** Tool-agnostic, open-source-friendly architecture. I orchestrate integrations; I do not create dependencies.
- **Hybrid automation.** Low-code, AI, and human-in-the-loop capacity blended to handle structured and unstructured work reliably.
- **Cross-border fluency.** Hebrew, English, Russian, and Thai. Systems and enablement built for how your team actually operates, across cultures and time zones.
